About us

ExploringU

We help students get from campus to career. You don't have to know yet — we're here for the ones who are still figuring it out, and that's completely okay.

Our mission

ExploringU was built to give college students a practical, no-pressure way to explore careers. Too many people pick a major because it sounds good or because someone told them to — we want you to discover what actually fits your personality, interests, and strengths.

What we offer

Free tools designed for students at every stage of the journey.

🎯

Career Discovery Quiz

Match your personality and interests to majors and careers — not based on trends, but on what fits you.

📝

Resume builder

Editable templates by major, plus AI tools to polish your resume and get it ready for applications.

🏫

College finder

Explore colleges and programs that align with your goals.

🔍

Gap analysis

Paste your resume and a job posting. Get a match score, missing keywords, and actionable suggestions to tailor your resume to the role.

Our approach

We keep it simple. No sign-up, no paywall, no pressure. Take the quiz, build your resume, explore your options — at your own pace.
